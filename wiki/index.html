EmDash Admin Wiki

Internal how-tos for understanding and customizing the EmDash admin UI. This information is not on the public web — it was reverse-engineered from the shipped @emdash-cms bundles (v0.16.1) and verified against source.

The one idea to take away: you don't customize the EmDash admin menu by editing the menu. You customize it by defining content collections. Each collection you declare in seed/seed.json automatically becomes its own labeled item in the sidebar.
